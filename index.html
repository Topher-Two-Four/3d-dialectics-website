Welcome to the website of the studio, 3D Dialectics!

We are 3D Dialectics, New Mexico's first-of-its-kind video game studio, bringing the game industry to the Land of Enchantment.

But, how?

I'm so glad you asked! As the people of New Mexico, we recognize how talented our diverse population is, and we are looking to get those talented folks involved in making games. We take our unique skillsets and experiences as New Mexican's, and we use them to create exciting games for the rest of the world to enjoy.

Like a beacon in the high desert, this is a call to all of you out there who want to make games but did not have the channels to do so. If you're interested, please contact us, we will be announcing projects and hiring soon!

We look forward to displaying the talent our great state of New Mexico has to offer, and mark my words, New Mexico will become the newest hotshot in the global games industry.
