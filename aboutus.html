About Us

3D Dialectics is a game studio in Albuquerque, NM, that seeks to advance research and development in game production. The studio was founded in 2022, with the mission of bringing the game industry to New Mexico. Our vision is rooted in inclusivity, diversity, and opportunity, shining light upon the unique experiences of our communities.
