About Us

  3D Dialectics is a game studio in Albuquerque, NM, that seeks to advance research and development in game production. Our studio was founded in 2022 by Christopher DeBonis, with the mission of bringing the game industry to New Mexico. Our vision is rooted in inclusivity, diversity, and opportunity, shining light upon the unique experiences of our communities.
